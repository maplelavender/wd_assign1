More on Gacha Games!

A gacha game is a video game where a main mechanic involves using a premium currency to obtain a random prize, which is usually a character.

Gacha is a random lottery-based mechanic and is a term that is named after the capsule vending machines in Japan, called Gachapon.

Most Gacha games are ARPGs (Action Role-Playing Game), but they can also be turn-based games, rhythm games, and tower defence games!
